商务口语集锦三

August 7, 2021 1-minute read
English
- Can you all take a look at your schedules?

    你们能看看你们的日程安排吗？

- Thursday mornings are usually the slowest around here.

    星期四上午总是这儿最闲的时候。

- We're going to need everybody's input on this project. So I'd like to fix a time to meet next week and hear what you all have to say.

    我们需要大家同心协力地投入到这个项目中来. 因此我想和大家定时间在下周开个会，听取各位的意见

- I do not have any problem with that. Let's set it up for 9:30 Wednesday morning. We can carry it over to Thursday if we need to.

    我没问题. 咱们就定在周三早上9:30吧，如有必要星期四可以继续.

- I wanted to see if I could arrange a meeting with you to discuss recruitment.

    我们是不是可以安排个时间讨论下招聘的问题.

- Absolutely.

    当然.

- Sounds good to me. You make up the invitations, I've got run. I've got a meeting in 5 minutes.

    我觉得不错，你来发邀请，我得走了，5分钟后我有个会要开。

- I could come in any day next week except for next Friday morning.

    除了星期五上午，下周哪天都行。

- I'm sorry, again, for the delay, but we should have everything completed by the time you get back.

    我再次对耽搁表示抱歉，但您下次回来时我们会把所有事情都办妥的。

- I certainly hope so.

    我当然希望如此了。
