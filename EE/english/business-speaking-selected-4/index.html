商务口语集锦四

August 7, 2021 1-minute read
English
- I think you should look over these figures again.

    我想你应该再把这些数字核实一遍

- There are some calculations that are a bit off.

    有些计算不太确切

- He'd like to know if you can send over those training manuals?

    他想知道您能否将那些培训手册发过去？

- Oh, tell him I'll leave them at his office tomorrow afternoon.

    噢，告诉他明天下午我把它们送到他的办公室

- I hate to do this to you, Alice, but I'm going to have to ask you to put in some more overtime.

    我不愿如此对你，可我不得不请你再加些班。

- Does it have to be this afternoon, Mr. Fairbanks? I've already made plans.

    非得在今天下午吗？我已经有安排了。

- Well, I would have preferred to do it today, but if you've already made plans, we can do it tomorrow.

    嗯，我希望能在今天做，可是如果你已经做了安排，我们可以明天做。

- I'd appreciate that, sir.

    如能这样我非常感激，先生。

- Albert, could you co sign this approval sheet? Accounting is getting really picky.

    你能也在这份批准书上签字吗？财务部变得越来越严格了。

- I know. They've already sent back two approvals I sent them.

    我知道，他们已经退回我送的两份批准书了。
